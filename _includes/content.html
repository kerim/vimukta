About Vimukta

Vimukta was founded by the producers of the documentary film, Please Don’t Beat Me, Sir! as a means to give something back to India's Denotified and Nomadic Tribes (DNTs). Our name comes from the term Vimukta Jati which is another name for DNTs. It also means “free” or “liberated” in Hindi.

A registered 501(c)(3) charity organization, donations to Vimukta are tax-deductible in the United States.
